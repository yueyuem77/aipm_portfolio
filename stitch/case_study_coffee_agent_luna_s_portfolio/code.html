ROLE AI Engineer + PM
TEAM Solo
TIMELINE 3 months

[PROBLEM_LOG]

Coffee shop owners face a binary crisis: they either overbuy perishable inventory—leading to massive waste—or understock during peak surges, losing critical revenue. Current tools are generic spreadsheets that fail to account for weather, local events, or neighborhood nuances.

Inefficiency Metric
22%

AVG. INVENTORY WASTE PER QUARTER

Narrative Arc

01_HYPOTHESIS

WE THOUGHT

A single global Prophet model could predict sales across all product categories using historical data alone.

02_DISCOVERY

DATA SHOWED

Separate patterns emerged: Pastries follow weekday commutes; Espresso follows weather; Merchandise follows events.

03_EXECUTION

SO WE DID

Architected a 3-model ensemble tailored to specific inventory decay rates and consumer behaviors.
